Sambet

June 02, 2014

The Sambet app allows sharing your soccer game tips with your friends. Just build different groups to see who gives the best tips for the soccer world championship 2014 - in an easy and fun way.

Sambet is a student project - therefore constructive feedback is always welcome! If you like Sambet, please like and share to support us.

Upcomming features:
+ View the tips of your friends
+ Pull to refresh

Sambet is available for Android and iOS.
